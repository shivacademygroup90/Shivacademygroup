About Shiv Academy Group

Shiv Academy Group is a Merchant Navy career guidance and training academy focused on helping students build a successful career in the maritime industry. We provide guidance for Merchant Navy courses, admission procedures, required documents, and career opportunities.

Why Choose Shiv Academy Group?

Career Guidance

Complete guidance from course selection to joining the maritime industry.

Merchant Navy Training

Support for students who want to start their journey in the shipping sector.

Placement Assistance

Career support and guidance for eligible candidates.

Our Mission

Our mission is to guide aspiring seafarers with proper information, career planning, and support to achieve their Merchant Navy goals.
